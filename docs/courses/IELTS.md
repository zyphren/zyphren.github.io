# 雅思写作 & 口语核心笔记

[!abstract] 笔记说明
本笔记基于雅思备考经验整理，涵盖写作主体段、结论段的结构与技巧，以及口语三部分的考试要点和答题策略。使用 callout 块突出不同类型的内容，便于快速查阅。

---

## 一、雅思写作：主体段落

### 1. 段落结构标准
- **文章结构**：四段式大作文
- **主体段数量**：2 个
- **每段长度**：5 句话 / 90–100 词

### 2. 两种写作方法

[!example] **方法 A：Firstly, Secondly, Finally**
**适用场景**：提出三个独立的论点
**核心技巧**：3 分钟快速规划三个论点

**示例（反对强制社区服务）**：
There are several reasons why I would argue against having compulsory community service for secondary school students. Firstly, the school curriculum is already full with important academic subjects, such as maths, science and languages. For example, I remember having an extremely busy timetable when I was at high school, and it would not have been possible to add to it. Secondly, students' performance in other subjects would be affected if valuable study time were taken by charity work or neighbourhood improvement schemes. Finally, I believe that teenage students would be reluctant to take part in any programme of obligatory work, and this could lead to poor motivation and even bad behaviour.

[!example] **方法 B：Idea, Explain, Example**
**适用场景**：深入探讨一个核心观点
**结构**：观点 → 解释 → 例子（例子位置可灵活调整）

**示例（支持自愿社区服务）**：
On the other hand, the opportunity to do voluntary community service could be extremely positive for high school students. By making these programmes optional, schools would ensure that only motivated students took part. These young people would gain valuable experience in an adult working environment, which could help to build their self-confidence and enhance their skills. Having such experience and skills on their CVs could greatly improve school leavers' career prospects. For example, a period of voluntary work experience might impress a university admissions officer or a future employer.

[!tip] **重要提示**
- **词汇加分**：连接词（Firstly, Finally 等）不会直接提高分数；**主题相关词汇**才是提分关键。
- **3 分钟计划**：应包括 **想法 + 词汇 + 结构**。

## 二、雅思写作：结论段

[!note] **两条黄金法则**
1.  **绝不引入新观点**：只总结和重申。
2.  **一句话结构**：通过转述引言段的观点来完成。

### 示例对比

[!info] **示例 1：监控摄像头**
- **引言**：It is true that video surveillance has become commonplace in many cities in recent years. While I understand that critics may see this as an invasion of privacy, I believe that there are more benefits than drawbacks.
- **结论**：In conclusion, I would argue that the advantages of using video security systems in public places do outweigh the disadvantages.

[!info] **示例 2：私立学校税收**
- **引言**：Some people believe that parents of children who attend private schools should not need to contribute to state schools through taxes. Personally, I completely disagree with this view.
- **结论**：In conclusion, I do not believe that any financial concessions should be made for people who choose private education.

[!info] **示例 3：人口老龄化**
- **引言**：It is true that people in industrialised nations can expect to live longer than ever before. Although there will undoubtedly be some negative consequences of this trend, societies can take steps to mitigate these potential problems.
- **结论**：In conclusion, various measures can be taken to tackle the problems that are certain to arise as the populations of countries grow older.

## 三、雅思口语：考试概述

[!summary] **时间分配**
- **总时长**：11–14 分钟
- **Part 1**：4–5 分钟
- **Part 2**：3–4 分钟（1 分钟准备 + 2 分钟作答）
- **Part 3**：4–5 分钟

[!tip] **备考要点**
- **知道预期**：了解考试流程 + 考官期待。
- **自然为王**：使用自然语言，不要刻意追求生僻词汇。
- **快速反应**：想到什么就立刻回答。
- **不要担心**：考官的表情/反应；你的观点是否足够深刻。

---

## 四、口语 Part 1：热身环节

[!info] **Part 1 特点**
- 4–5 分钟，约 10–11 个问题。
- 3 个话题领域（工作/学习、家乡、爱好等）。
- 话题通常比较简单，甚至显得幼稚。

[!success] **答题技巧**
- **不要停顿思考**：展示流利度最重要。
- **不要纠结词汇语法**：Part 1 无法决定高分。
- **公式**：**答案 + 理由**。
- **结束信号**：自信停顿 + 微笑注视考官。

---

## 五、口语 Part 2：人物与地点

### 1. 常见话题
- **人物**：朋友/家人/名人/孩子/影响你的人。
- **地点**：到访过的城市、想去的地方、历史景点、商店/餐厅/市场。

### 2. 答题结构

[!example] **描述人物**
- **Who**：外貌 + 性格
- **What he/she does**：职业/日常
- **When/How/Where you met**：相遇背景
- **Why you like this person**：核心原因

[!example] **描述地点**
- **Where it is**
- **What it looks like**
- **What you can do there**
- **Why you like/want to go there**

### 3. 主题法准备

[!quote] **核心策略**：为每类话题预设一个核心主题，围绕主题升级词汇。

**示例：以“有趣的城市”为主题**
- **基础词**：fun, busy, interesting
- **升级词**：bustling, hectic, thriving, cosmopolitan, multicultural, fascinating, sightseeing, entertainment

### 4. 练习方法

[!note] **三步法**
1.  选择话题 → 直接开口说并录音。
2.  使用语音转文字工具 → 整理成文本。
3.  修改润色 → 吸收更好的词汇和表达。

[!warning] **关键心态**
考试中需要根据实际情况调整和即兴发挥，不可能完全照搬准备的内容。
