# Speaking part 2:person

## Describe a person

3 to 4 miutes
1 minute to prepare
speak for 2 minutes
task card
describe...
## Before the exam

Prepare for common topic areas
Vocabulary and ideas are the key
## Main topic areas:

Person,Place,Object
Event,Activity,Favourites
## Structure:Person

who (appearance,personality)
what he/she does
when how where you met
why you like this person
## Preparation tip

think of a 'theme'for any person (friend,family,famous,child etc.)
start with easy adjectives
search for better words and phrases
>Theme is core feature, the adjectives should surround the core feature

Practise workflow:

Choose a topic👉Speak out and record👉 voice-to-word transition tool👉improve the word

Start from the anwers your brain think at frist time, and improve from it, the adoptation needed will be min.
